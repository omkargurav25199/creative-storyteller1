Once upon a time, in a cozy cave high in the mountains, lived a tiny purple dragon named Ember. All the other dragons could breathe magnificent fire, but when Ember tried, delicate flowers of all colors bloomed from her mouth instead. She felt a little embarrassed, wishing she could be like the others. 

One sunny afternoon, Ember was flying through the forest when she saw a little bunny with a hurt paw. Gently, she breathed out her special flowers onto the bunny's paw, and to her surprise, the bunny's paw began to heal! Ember realized her unique gift could help others. 

Soon, Ember had created the most beautiful flower garden the dragon kingdom had ever seen, filled with vibrant blossoms and sweet scents. All the other dragons gathered to admire her amazing garden, and Ember, with her flower-shaped mark shining, felt incredibly proud to be exactly who she was.
